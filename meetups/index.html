meetup FAQs

Where can I find info on the next meetup?

We post the meetup details to our Twitter and our Discord. Find us there!


Where are the meetups held?

Meetups are currently being held at The Brisbane Square Library, usually in the Tiered Theatrette. The Brisbane Square Library is within the CDB and close to both bus and train stations.


Do I need to RSVP?

Nope! This is a very casual meetup. Most people just come to the ones that they are able to.


Do I have to stay for the whole 3 hour long meetup?

Not at all. We book the area for the full time but most people end up coming and going as they please. It’s very chill.


What do I bring?

You can bring anything you have been working on, at any stage of completion or polish. Feel free to bring a laptop to show off work or get help from others. If you have a lot of gear, we suggest bringing a powerboard.


What if I don’t have anything to show?

Squiggly River is open to anyone, regardless of experience. You are absolutely welcome to just bring yourself, see what others have been working on and have a chat.


How can I find the group?

We will always include which room we are in within the event details.


Is the Brisbane Square Library accessible?

The Brisbane Square Library is fully wheelchair accessible. Full accessibility details on coucil libraries here.

Food and drinks?

The Brisbane Square Library has a kitchenette that can be used and it is within walking distance of a variety of cafes.
