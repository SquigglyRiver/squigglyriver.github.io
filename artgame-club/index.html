What is Artgame Club?

Every month we pick a game and discuss it at in the following meetup. Games are nominated, voted on and chosen within our Discord. We focus on free or inexpensive games with a playtime of anywhere between a few minutes to a few hours. The weirder the better! It's a great opportunity to explore some lesser known games and hopefully it will serve as inspiration too!


Past games:

  • Even the Stars by Pol Clarissou
  • Symphony of Seven Souls by Chris Evry
  • Glitchhikers by Silverstring Media
